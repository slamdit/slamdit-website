About

Building the future of small business automation

I'm a founder and full-stack developer who believes every small business deserves the same automation advantages that enterprise companies take for granted. I specialize in AI-powered tools that streamline real business processes — not just prototypes, but production systems that people use every day.

My current focus is the resale and secondhand economy, where I'm building tools that handle everything from AI-powered pricing and multi-platform listing to estate sale operations and peer-to-peer agreements. Every tool I build is designed to eliminate manual work and let business owners focus on what they're good at.

I work with React, Supabase, Claude AI, and modern edge function architectures to ship fast and iterate constantly. If your business has a process that feels like it should be automated — it probably should be, and I can help.
