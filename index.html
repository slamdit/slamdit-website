My Story

From Corporate Cubicle to Freedom

No victim mentality here — just gratitude, grit, and a whole lot of joy.

After 20 years in corporate, my position was eliminated. And you know what? It was the best thing that ever happened to me. I mean that. No bitterness, no looking back — just pure, overwhelming gratitude for this incredible second chance at actually living.

My current situation is a GIFT, and I thank God every single day for it. I have more energy now than I've ever had and an inner peace I didn't know could exist.

Here's the thing nobody tells you about leaving corporate: all those years of solving business bottlenecks, automating clunky processes, and wrangling SharePoint into doing things it probably shouldn't have been doing? That wasn't wasted time. It was training. It positioned me to build real solutions using AI — and now I get to do it on my own terms.

I'd honestly rather be outside in nature than in front of my computer (and if you know me, you know that's saying something, because I really love building things). But that's the whole point — life isn't meant to be spent sitting behind a desk all day. There's a whole world out there. I want to help you find your way to it.

Faith and family are my number one motivators. My spiritual wellbeing has transformed since this journey started, and I've discovered an inner knowing that life has so much more to offer when you stop living on someone else's schedule.

I'm here to share what I've learned, build things that help people, and spread a little encouragement along the way. Whether you chose to leave corporate or got pushed — welcome. You're in the right place. And yes, I'm also hilarious and incredibly humble. (You'll just have to take my word for it. 😂)
